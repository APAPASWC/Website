APACEvotes

APACEvotes strives to increase access and participation in electoral and civic affairs by registering, educating and protecting Asian Pacific Islander American voters.


The WA Census Alliance is a coalition of POC organizations of which APACE is a part of. One of our campaigns is a postcard campaign. We had folks fill out this postcard which we would send to their respective legislators. This postcard campaign is to reinforce the $15 million ask from the state budget so we can adequately fund community based organizations to Get Out the Count. Luckily, the senate recently came out with a $15 million proposal. The data entry is just to get the postcards uploaded and sent to their correct legislators. Easy work, just a bit time consuming.
